About OpsWallah

Practical Industry Learning for Non-Technical Graduates

An Independent Career Readiness Initiative — Created by Vimal Singh

OpsWallah helps students, graduates and early-career professionals understand how Banking, FinTech, Excel and business operations work in real situations—not only through formulas or theoretical definitions.

Why OpsWallah Exists

Many graduates—especially those from non-technical backgrounds—complete their education without enough practical exposure to business operations, workplace communication and industry processes. OpsWallah exists to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical industry understanding.

Created by Vimal Singh

OpsWallah has been independently conceptualised and created by Vimal Singh, who brings more than 15 years of professional experience across Banking, FinTech and Digital Payment Operations.

His experience spans practical operational areas including payments, cards, reconciliation, refunds, partner management, process controls, reporting, automation and audit readiness.

Banking & Payment Operations Cards & NCMC BBPS & Digital Payments Reconciliation & Refunds Vendor, Partner & Process Management Controls, Reporting, Automation & Audit Readiness

Do Not Just Memorise the Process. Understand Why It Exists.

Every important topic is taught through a practical learning journey — from the real-world Problem it solves, through its Evolution, the Product and Process built around it, into day-to-day Operations, Risk, Compliance, Control and Audit — so you understand where it's headed, not just how to repeat it.
